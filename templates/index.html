Introduction

CryptoSight is a Telegram bot made for cryptocurrency traders. This bot comes packed with many cool features and customizable options, such as automatic profit calculations whenever a trade is executed.
We have recently partnered with Gunbot, so you can find more details about our product here.

Features

  • Fully customized keyboard
  • Support for multiple exchanges
  • Detailed overview of trade history and portfolio
  • Calculations such as win rate, Sharpe's ratio and many more..
  • Choose between light and dark themes
  • Directly integrated into Gunbot
  • And many more features...
